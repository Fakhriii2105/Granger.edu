Granger University Proposes Green City Concept for Sustainable Urban Development

Green University (Granger U) has unveiled its Green City Concept, a visionary plan to create an eco-friendly urban space. The concept includes energy-efficient buildings, renewable energy systems, sustainable transportation, and green public spaces, aiming to reduce carbon emissions and improve urban living. “We envision a city where sustainability is at its core,” said Head of Environmental Engineering. The proposal has drawn attention from local officials and environmental groups, with Granger U planning to collaborate with experts for further development. This concept could set a new standard for sustainable city design.
